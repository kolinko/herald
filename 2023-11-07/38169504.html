Zombie Apocalypse at Bored Ape Yacht Club? Exclusive: Vision Loss Epidemic Strikes Crypto's Elite!

By Lenny "Deadline" Dawson - Staff Writer

Date: 23 Apr 2023

Crypto calamity strikes the elite at ApeFest as attendees fall victim to an ocular onslaught, reminiscent of a sci-fi horror flick. Reports suggest a mysterious wave of vision loss and searing eye pain, a cryptic plight following revelry amidst lasers and blacklight. Is this the new price of blockchain bragging rights?

As one unlucky high-roller recalled, "I awakened to the apocalypse in my eyeballs!" What was intended to be an evening celebrating the peak of virtual vanity, transformed in a blink into a dystopian nightmare. Victims describe symptoms ranging from mild irritation to photophobic afflictions perilously close to a comic book mutation. Medical professionals involved suggested the event's visual fiesta trafficked in more than just hype – it was a veritable buffet of ultraviolet mayhem that feasted unceremoniously upon their retinas.

Meanwhile, Yuga Labs, the mastermind behind the beleaguered Bored Ape Yacht Club and proprietors of the pandemonium, have yet to flash a signal of distress or empathy. As chatter swells within the community, speculation is rife; could this act of unintentional cyber-sabotage be a metaphorical manifestation of the volatile market, rendering its wealthiest participants blind not only to risk but now, quite literally, to the world itself?

Let's face reality – the Hacker Herald might embellish its stories with a smidgen of hyperbole, but this tale of visual vengeance is as close to the truth as it gets.
